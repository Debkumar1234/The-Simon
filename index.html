Here are some rules for the Simon game:

  • At the start of the game, a key lights up randomly and makes a sound.
  • The player must press the same key.
  • Simon then turns on the same light again and a second light, also randomly.
  • Any player who makes a mistake or takes longer than five seconds to repeat a signal is eliminated from the game.
  • The goal is to repeat the longest sequence of signals correctly.

Simon is an electronic game that tests short-term memory skills.
